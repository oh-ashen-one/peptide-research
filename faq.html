Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our research peptides, ordering process, and quality standards.

All peptides in our catalog meet or exceed 99% purity as verified by independent third-party HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) analysis. Certificate of Analysis documentation is available on request for any compound and batch.

Store lyophilized (freeze-dried) peptides at -20°C or below in a dry, light-protected environment. Properly stored lyophilized peptides maintain stability for extended periods. Once reconstituted, store at 2-8°C and use within the validated stability window. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.

Reconstitute with bacteriostatic water or sterile water for injection. Add the solvent slowly along the inside wall of the vial — do not shake or agitate vigorously. Allow the lyophilized powder to dissolve gently. For multiple-use protocols, aliquot the reconstituted solution to avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles.
