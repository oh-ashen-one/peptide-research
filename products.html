BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157) is a pentadecapeptide consisting of 15 amino acids, derived from a partial sequence of human gastric juice protein. Research has extensively investigated its cytoprotective and regenerative properties in gastrointestinal models, including gastric ulcer healing, inflammatory bowel disease models, and esophageal damage repair. Additional preclinical studies have explored tendon-to-bone healing, muscle crush injury recovery, and neuroprotective effects. BPC-157 has demonstrated activity through multiple proposed mechanisms including nitric oxide system modulation and VEGF pathway involvement.
