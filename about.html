Handling Protocols

Storage & Reconstitution

Proper handling ensures compound integrity for your research. Follow these guidelines for all peptide products.

Storage

Long-Term Storage

Store lyophilized peptides at -20°C or below in a dry environment. Protect from light. Properly stored lyophilized peptides maintain stability for extended periods. Do not freeze-thaw repeatedly after reconstitution.

Reconstitution

Preparation

Reconstitute with bacteriostatic water or sterile water for injection. Add solvent slowly along the vial wall — do not agitate vigorously. Allow the peptide to dissolve gently. Reconstituted solutions should be stored at 2-8°C and used within the validated stability window.

Handling

Best Practices

Use aseptic technique when handling all peptide compounds. Work in a clean environment. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles of reconstituted solutions. Aliquot reconstituted peptides for multiple-use protocols to preserve stability.
